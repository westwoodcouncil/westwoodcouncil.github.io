North Westwood Neighborhood Council

Announcement

Want to impact your community by serving on the North Westwood Neighborhood Council?

Although there were 26 candidates for 19 seats on Thursday's election, due to 2 candidates changing their stakeholder status , one Faculty and one at-large seat are still open!

The At-large seat is open to anyone who lives, works, attends school, or otherwise has a substantial and ongoing interest within North Westwood.

The Faculty seat is, "Open to stakeholders who work at a university or college within the North Westwood boundaries in a capacity in which they spend the majority of their time conducting research and/or teaching. Employee need not be currently employed as longas they have been employed within the past 180 days and intend to be re-employed within the next 180 days."

If you are interested in serving, please send a brief email to northwestwoodcouncil@gmail.com explaining your qualifications and why you'd like to serve, and be prepared to attend our June 10th meeting, where the board will make its selection. Thanks!

Committee Announcement

Want to get involved with the North Westwood Neighborhood Council? Positions are open for both elected council members and also non-elected stakeholders to serve on the committees that play a crucial role in shaping the policies of our Council and engaging our Community. If interested, please send an email by June 5th at 5pm to northwestwoodcouncil@gmail.com briefly noting which committees you'd like to serve on, why you'd like to serve, and what perspectives and experiences you'd be bringing into this role.

Budget and Finance Committee (Will monitor the council's budget and make recommendations for how the council's funds should be spent, including helping to facilitate the Neighborhood Purpose Grant program which will fund projects serving stakeholders in our community.)

The Outreach and Communications Committee (Will work to make stakeholders aware of the council's activities and keep the council engaged with the communities it serves).

The Planning and Land Use Committee (Will review proposed developments and businesses seeking to operate or adjust their operations in our community. Will also make recommendations on what zoning or ordinance changes could improve North Westwood.)

The Transportation and Safety Committee (Will work to make North Westwood a safe and accessible place to get around and enjoy.)

The Elections Committee (Will work to effectively engage North Westwood's stakeholders in each election.)

Community Activities and Projects Committee (Will work to plan and support events and projects that engage or serve the North Westwood Community).
